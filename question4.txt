Tag:

HTML tag is used to define the structure of element of our webpage,tags are usually 
consist of opening and closing tag,web browser display the content according to the tag we have 
used.some tag just have  the opening tag not the closing tag.

Example:

<h1> This is heading one tag </h1>
<p> This is paragraph tag </p>
Here Above <h1> and <p> are called opening tags whereas </h1> and </p> are called closing tags. 
 
Element:

Element consist of opening tag ,closing tag and the content between the tags.
Everything from the opening tag to the closing tag is called an element in html.

Example:

<p> This is an element </p>
This above whole line is called an element.
